For this lab, with the use of powershell I automated the provisioning of Users and Groups. I implemented a tiered Organizational Unit (OU) structure to facilitate Administrative Partitioning. 
By separating Admin_Accounts and Service_Accounts from standard Users, we can apply more restrictive Group Policy Objects (GPOs), such as enforcing MFA or restricting login hours, which are core tenets of Privileged Access Management (PAM).
